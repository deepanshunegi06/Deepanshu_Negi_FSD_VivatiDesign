About Vivati

Vivati is a project of passion with a purpose to provide innovation, credible quality and life service for the discerning client base.

Drawing on the vision of “Think, Big, Start Small and Innovate Constantly”, we set up an expert in-house team of professional furniture designers, machine operators, carpenters, and upholsterers, complementing our European furniture machines to manufacture bespoke and modular furniture of high standards for residential and commercial spaces.

Quality and Craftsmanship

At Vivati Design, we value crafting exceptional, high-quality pieces that elevate the beauty and functionality of any space. Our team of skilled artisans and designers work tirelessly to bring unique and bespoke furniture designs to life, using only the finest materials and time-honoured techniques.

Customization & Personalization

We are a bespoke furniture design manufacturer that specializes in creating one-of-a-kind, custom pieces that reflect our clients' individual styles and personalities. From modern kitchens & luxurious wardrobes to residential & office furniture, our expert designers and craftsmen work closely with clients to bring their unique vision to life, ensuring a perfect blend of form, function, and flair.

Meet Our Directors

Vivek Chauhan

A former Indian Army officer (20+ years) and UN performance manager (4+ years), he leverages his HR and strategic planning skills to lead operations and drive the company’s mission of delivering exceptional furniture solutions.

Rati Kuntal

With a keen eye for design and a talent for understanding client aspirations, Rati crafts bespoke luxury furniture that exceeds expectations. Her people-centric approach extends to both clients and staff, fostering a collaborative environment that drives innovation.
