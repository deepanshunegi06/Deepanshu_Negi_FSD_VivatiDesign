A Glimpse Of Our Factory

Located in sector 68 Gurugram, on an independent one acre plot, the factory has repurposed 20,000 square ft. of warehouse space into a top-class Furniture Manufacturing facility. Our production stands apart as we have capacity and capability with processes driven assembly lines that ensure we can work in both B2B and B2C segments.


We therefore are able to provide a one stop solution for all furniture design and installation needs, enabling our clients to create the desired living spaces.
